The Problem

Over 75% of Kenya's farmers lose up to 40% of crop yield annually due to poor soil monitoring, delayed pest detection, and no access to precision agriculture tools — which cost tens of thousands of dollars.

Our Solution
  • Autonomous robot patrols fields 24/7, collects soil & environmental data
  • AI crop advisor gives real-time recommendations via a web dashboard
  • Computer vision detects crop pest and disease from camera feed
  • GPS field mapping tracks robot position and logs field zones
